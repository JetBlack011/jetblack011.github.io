  • Heegaard Splittings. A talk on Heegaard Splittings for week 1 of a first-year grad student topology bootcamp (fall 2024)
  • Multisections of 4-Manifolds. A talk on results from the 2022 UGA REU for the Texas Undergraduate Math Conference (spring 2022)
